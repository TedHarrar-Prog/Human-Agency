# Project Background

## Who I Am

Ted Harrar. I'm applying for an executive role at Human Agency (humanagency.com), leading one of their new ventures. As part of the application, they've asked for an example of something I've built with AI.

## What This Project Is

A fun, single-page landing page: a cartoon car race game. A big "Start Race" button launches 5 cartoon cars around a track, each piloted by a real member of the Human Agency team. The winner is always random. The goal is to make whoever at Human Agency clicks the button laugh, and to make it obvious this was built with AI, specifically for them, as a lighthearted show of what I can build.

This is a different tone than my other Claude Code projects. It should feel playful and a little silly, not polished or corporate.
